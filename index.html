About

A developer who
thinks in products.

I’m a Frontend Developer and Product Manager based in France. I build web applications from the ground up and lead products from the first user interview through to launch — two disciplines that, in my experience, make each other sharper.

On the engineering side, my work centres on React, TypeScript and PWA architecture. I care about performance, accessibility and the kind of interface that disappears — one where the user never has to think about the tool, only the task.

On the product side, I’ve run full product lifecycles for self-funded independent projects — writing PRDs, conducting user research, managing risk registers and coordinating cross-functional delivery through Agile sprints. I also completed a frontend developer internship at Unilab, working on production React applications alongside a cross-functional team.

I’m most engaged at the boundary between design thinking and engineering rigour — where specifications become real and assumptions become features.
