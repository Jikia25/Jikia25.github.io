About

A developer who thinks
like a product manager.

I’m Kristina Jikia, a Frontend Developer and Product Manager based in France, originally from Tbilisi, Georgia. I bridge the gap between engineering and product — building interfaces that are both technically sound and strategically intentional.

My frontend work focuses on React, TypeScript, and PWA architecture — crafting fast, accessible, production-ready applications. I care deeply about the user experience that emerges from clean code.

On the product side, I’ve led full product lifecycles at 10x IS College and contributed to product development at Unilab — writing PRDs, conducting user research, running sprint planning, and managing cross-functional delivery from concept through launch.

I’m at my best at the intersection of design thinking and engineering rigour — where good products are actually built.
